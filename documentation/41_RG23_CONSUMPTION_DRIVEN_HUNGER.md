\# 41 — RG23 Consumption-Driven Hunger



\## Purpose



This reference generation transforms Hunger Pressure from a binary condition into a constitutionally reconstructed biological state.



Rather than responding only to food depletion, Hunger Pressure is reconstructed from the proportion of nutritional requirements satisfied during the current constitutional revision.
