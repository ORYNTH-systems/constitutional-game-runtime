\# 33 — EP-004 Water Cycle



\## Purpose



This document defines Environmental Process EP-004: Water Cycle.



The Water Cycle converts atmospheric and seasonal environmental state into usable water availability.



\## Constitutional Principle



Water availability is not static.



It is governed environmental state derived from climate, season, weather, rainfall, and temperature.



\## Inputs



EP-004 evaluates:



\- Climate

\- Season

\- Weather

\- Rainfall

\- Temperature

\- Current water availability

\- Environmental revision



\## Outputs



EP-004 may update:



\- Water Availability

\- Environmental Evidence

\- Environmental Revision
