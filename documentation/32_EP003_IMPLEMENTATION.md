\# 32 — EP-003 Climate Implementation



\## Purpose



This document defines the implementation architecture for Environmental Process EP-003.



EP-003 introduces persistent constitutional climate as the governing constraint of the Environmental System.



Unlike seasonal and weather state, climate changes infrequently and establishes the long-term environmental identity of the world.

\# Architectural Significance



EP-003 completes the foundational environmental hierarchy.



The Constitutional Runtime now contains persistent environmental identity (Climate), cyclical environmental evolution (Season), and short-term environmental reconstruction (Weather).



This layered architecture allows future environmental, biological, settlement, economic, and governance systems to consume shared constitutional state without requiring changes to the execution architecture.



\---



\# Summary



EP-003 establishes constitutional climate as the highest-level environmental constraint.



Climate defines the environmental identity of the world while Seasons provide cyclical variation and Weather reconstructs immediate atmospheric conditions. Together they form the foundational environmental substrate upon which the remainder of the Constitutional Runtime evolves.
