\# 43 — RG23 Hunger-Driven Health



\## Purpose



This document defines RG23-D: Hunger-Driven Health.



Population Health is reconstructed from accumulated Hunger Pressure, which is now derived from proportional food consumption.



\## Dependency Chain



Climate → Season → Weather → Water → Soil → Food Growth → Food Consumption → Hunger Pressure → Health



\## Principle



Health is not an isolated population attribute.



It is downstream constitutional state produced by ecological adequacy or deprivation.



\## Inputs



CP-004 evaluates:



\- Current Health

\- Current Hunger Pressure

\- Latest Consumption Ratio



\## Outputs



CP-004 updates:



\- Population Health



\## Summary



RG23-D completes the propagation of environmental scarcity into biological integrity.
