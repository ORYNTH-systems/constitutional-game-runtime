\# 25 — RG22 Environmental System



\## Purpose



This document defines the Environmental Constitutional System.



The Environmental System introduces external world conditions that influence biological, settlement, economic, and governance processes.



\## Constitutional Principle



The environment is not background scenery.



It is constitutional state.



Environmental conditions affect what can grow, what can move, what can be consumed, what can be built, and what can remain admissible.
