\# 21 — CP-004 Population Health



\## Purpose



This document defines Constitutional Process CP-004: Population Health.



Population Health represents the constitutional condition of a population as it responds to sustained environmental stress.



Health is not an independent variable.



It emerges from the interaction of constitutional processes operating upon the population and the world.



\---



\# Constitutional Principle



Population health is a governed constitutional state.



It changes only through admissible constitutional processes and contributes directly to future world evolution.



\---



\# Purpose of the Process



CP-004 converts accumulated constitutional stress into measurable population condition.



It establishes the first constitutional feedback mechanism between resources and population viability.



\---



\# Inputs



The process evaluates:



\- Population

\- Hunger Pressure

\- Available Resources

\- Current Population Health



Future generations may additionally incorporate:



\- Disease

\- Climate

\- Water Availability

\- Shelter

\- Medicine

\- Governance Quality



\---



\# Outputs



The process updates:



\- Population Health



Future constitutional processes may depend upon this value.



\---

\# Summary



CP-004 establishes Population Health as a constitutional state variable linking resource availability to long-term population evolution.



It is the first process that transforms constitutional stress into constitutional condition, enabling higher-order biological, ecological, and societal feedback loops.
