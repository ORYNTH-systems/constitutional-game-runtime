\# 28 — EP-001 Seasons Implementation



\## Purpose



This document defines the implementation architecture for Environmental Process EP-001.



EP-001 establishes deterministic seasonal evolution within the Constitutional Environmental System.



Seasonal progression is governed by constitutional simulation time and produces immutable environmental revisions.



\---



\# Architectural Position



EP-001 is the root process of the Environmental System.



```text

Environmental Model

&#x20;       │

&#x20;       ▼

EP-001 Seasons

&#x20;       │

&#x20;       ▼

Future Environmental Processes

```



Every future environmental process depends upon seasonal constitutional state.



\---



\# Execution Pipeline



Each constitutional simulation tick executes:



```text

Simulation Clock

&#x20;       │

&#x20;       ▼

Current Season

&#x20;       │

&#x20;       ▼

Transition Evaluation

&#x20;       │

&#x20;       ▼

Governance Decision

&#x20;       │

&#x20;       ▼

Season Update

&#x20;       │

&#x20;       ▼

Environmental Evidence

&#x20;       │

&#x20;       ▼

Environmental Revision

```



\---



\# Constitutional State



Revision 1 introduces:



\- Season

\- Season Index

\- Day Within Season



Future revisions may extend the model without changing the execution architecture.



\---



\# Seasonal Progression



Initial deterministic cycle:



```text

Spring

&#x20;  │

&#x20;  ▼

Summer

&#x20;  │

&#x20;  ▼

Autumn

&#x20;  │

&#x20;  ▼

Winter

&#x20;  │

&#x20;  ▼

Spring

```



Each transition creates a new immutable environmental revision.



\---



\# Governance



EP-001 evaluates:



\- Current simulation day

\- Current season

\- Constitutional clock



If transition conditions are satisfied:



\- Update season

\- Reset seasonal day counter

\- Record environmental evidence



Otherwise:



\- Preserve current season



\---



\# Constitutional Evidence



Every execution records:



\- Previous season

\- Updated season

\- Simulation tick

\- Environmental revision

\- Governance result



Evidence becomes part of immutable constitutional history.



\---



\# Determinism



Seasonal evolution contains no randomness.



Identical constitutional histories always produce identical seasonal histories.



This guarantees replayability, benchmarking, and constitutional verification.



\---



\# Future Expansion



Future reference generations may introduce:



\- Variable season length

\- Hemisphere support

\- Planetary tilt

\- Orbital mechanics

\- Regional climates



These additions extend environmental fidelity without altering constitutional execution.



\---



\# Summary



EP-001 establishes the first executable Environmental Process.



Rather than functioning as a calendar, seasonal progression becomes governed constitutional state that future environmental, biological, settlement, economic, and governance systems consume through deterministic execution.
