\# 27 — EP-001 Seasons



\## Purpose



This document defines Environmental Process EP-001: Seasons.



Seasons provide the primary long-term environmental cycle governing biological productivity, climate variation, resource regeneration, and future environmental evolution.



Rather than serving as cosmetic world decoration, seasons are constitutional environmental state.



\---



\# Constitutional Principle



Seasonal progression is deterministic.



Every constitutional world advances through seasonal state according to constitutional time rather than scripted events.



All future environmental processes derive from the current seasonal state.



\---



\# Constitutional Responsibility



EP-001 is responsible for:



\- Maintaining seasonal state

\- Determining seasonal transitions

\- Providing environmental context for subordinate environmental processes

\- Recording seasonal constitutional evidence
